---
description: Bans a user but it's fun (and slightly spammy)
---

# a!funban &lt;user&gt; \[reason\]

### Examples

```text
a!funban @random breaking the rulez
```

```text
a!funban @random not partying hard enough
```

```text
a!funban 111372124383428608 being too cool
```
