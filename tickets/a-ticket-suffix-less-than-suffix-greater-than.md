---
description: >-
  Adds a suffix to a ticket, useful for knowing what's happening and organising
  things.
---

# a!ticket suffix &lt;suffix&gt;

### Examples

```text
a!ticket suffix open
```

```text
a!ticket suffix closed
```

```text
a!ticket suffix missing-info
```

```text
a!ticket suffix completed
```
