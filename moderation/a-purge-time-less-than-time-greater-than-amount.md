---
description: Purge messages from the last time. time should be something like 1h or 3m.
---

# a!purge time &lt;time&gt; \[amount\]

### Examples

```text
a!purge time 1h3m
```

```text
a!purge time 2m
```

```text
a!purge time 5s
```
