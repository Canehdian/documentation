---
description: Purges messages that includes your term  Supports wild*cards.
---

# a!purge includes &lt;term&gt; \[amount\]

### Examples

```text
a!purge includes xd rawr 10
```

```text
a!purge includes rawr
```

```text
a!purge includes rawr*xd
```

```text
a!purge includes a*z
```

```text
a!purge includes 10
```
