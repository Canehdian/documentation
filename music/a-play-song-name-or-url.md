# a!play \[song name \| url\]

### Examples

```text
a!play lil dicky - professional rapper
```

```text
a!play https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ
```

```text
a!play https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=W4ocPPhtglU&list=PLoXL8KeHXDAvYjd8SNhPkT7ob3LvbqTEz
```
